HERA KURIBIKE WIGIRE

This project consissts of identifying small vendors, mainly made of women,... widows, teen mothers from poor families and all other people that are financially challenged, we reach out to them, mobilize them to form tontines(saving groups), we pay for their community based health insurance and their family members. Then, we train them about entreprenuership, and give them small capital to carry out their small commercial acivities of their choice and more importantly we mentor them all along the journey.

FIGHTING TEENAGE PREGNANCIES AND REHABILITATION OF TEEN MOTHERS

In this project we carry out numerous activities responding to 3 dimensions of interventions:...

  1. Preventing Phase

    2. for the Preventing phase, we do activities aiming at inhibiting causes of teenage pregnancies through sensitizations of the mass, traing of the youths, children structures and parents sensitizing to own the battle against this scourge. We at Riviere de la joie Foundation call it, "Dangerous animal we ought to attack together as one." we use the media both classic and social media platforms to amplify the voice and make it heard by the masses

  2. Responding Phase

    3. we do train the victims and do the Mentorship program to mentor them, and help revive their hopes that due to pregnancy, one gets discouraged and have her hope for bright future fade, it's against that backdrop, we do Mentorship to restore their hope and help them catchup with their visions and dreams.

  3. Reporting Phase

    4. We do the alert system, named listing points and YOU SEE SOMETHING YOU SAY SOMETHING approach tailored to gather information about teenage pregancies and suspicious behaviour that can lead to teenage pregnancy from a family to the community reach. we collaborate with the government structures that have children's right into their mandate Police, RIB, at sector level.
