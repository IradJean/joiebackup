Riviere de la Joie Foundation

Since its inception in 2021, through her Vision Bearer and Godfather Mr Jeffrey C. Obiri, who visited Rwanda in the said year , Rivière de la Joie Foundation is a nonprofit organization whose focus includes vulnerable people, partic...ularly young people, women, widows and those with disabilities and all those living in situations of risk, in rural and remote areas and peri urban settings. RIVIÈRE DE LA JOIE cultivate the culture of self-reliance and self-belonging through empowerment, and with overall purpose of instilling into people the culture of love , kindness and compassion that seem to fade into our societies, The Foundation provides support on Sexual and Reproductive Health Rights, livelihood/food security, sports/talent promotions, Peace and security, Quality Education and Mentorship for young people enabling them to live their full potentials. Our approach involves providing a platform for the vulnerable people ,particularly young people, women( including widows ) those with disabilities and those living in situations of risks; to realize their hidden potentials through and by employing key strategies: Mentorship, Vocational Trainings , Economic empowerment, Community engagement and human rights activities, Community Outreach Programs , and so forth....

Our recent Projects

This project consissts of identifying small vendors, mainly made of women, widows, teen mothers from poor families and all other people that are financially challenged, we reach out to them, mobilize them to form tontines(saving groups), we pay for their community based health insurance and their family members.
